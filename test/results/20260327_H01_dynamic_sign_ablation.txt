- Replacing frozen S(phi0) by dynamic S(phi) is the strongest improvement found so far.
- The gain is systematic on all 12 irregular regular-grid case-steps for both numerical and origin.
- This does not prove that the paper used dynamic S(phi), because the text describes S(phi0); it does show that the current frozen-sign implementation is a major source of the remaining gap.
