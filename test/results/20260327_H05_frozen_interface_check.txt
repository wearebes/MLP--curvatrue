Conclusion:
- sampled nodes do not have |S0| close to zero; the mean |S0| is around 0.45 to 0.51
- patch nodes are even less frozen, with mean |S0| around 0.69 to 0.73
- phi on the sampled patches changes by O(1e-3), which is not negligible relative to the original phi0-to-exact gap
- reinitialization moves the local patch significantly closer to exact-SDF, rather than leaving it untouched
- therefore the frozen-interface explanation is weakened and should not be treated as the primary cause
